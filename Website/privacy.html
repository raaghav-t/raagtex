Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Raagtex handles information when you use the Raagtex app. Raagtex is designed as a local-first writing and PDF preview tool for LaTeX projects.

Information We Collect

Raagtex does not collect personal information from you and does not require an account. The app does not operate a remote backend service for document storage or user profiles.

Local Files and Device Data

Raagtex accesses files and folders that you choose from your device so you can edit and compile your LaTeX projects. Project files remain on your device unless you choose to move or share them yourself.

  • Recent project references and app preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Compile logs and diagnostics are generated locally as part of normal app functionality.
  • Raagtex does not upload your project contents to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Raagtex does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not sell your data. Apple may process limited data through App Store infrastructure according to Apple policies.

Children's Privacy

Raagtex is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: contact@raagtex.com
